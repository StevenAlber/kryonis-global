Operating Status & Proof

Current state of KRYONIS Global, BCCS and BioClearing.

I · Purpose of this page

What is live, what is documented,
and what is not being offered.

Serious counterparties should not have to infer the state of an architecture from its language. This page distinguishes, component by component: what is live today, what exists as documentation, what has been deployed where verifiable, what remains planned, what has been reviewed internally rather than independently audited, and what is not being offered to the public. Where a component's status cannot be evidenced in public materials, it is marked as pending, internal, or to be verified — not asserted.

Nothing on this page is legal, financial, or investment advice, and nothing on this page constitutes an offer or solicitation of any kind.

II · Executive status note

A strategic architecture studio
with a deployed protocol signal.

KRYONIS Global is live as a strategic architecture and protocol documentation studio: doctrine, hypothesis registry, and institutional analysis, published and operating at kryonis.global.

The commissions layer is live as a commercial advisory and documentation pathway — four fixed-scope, fixed-price instruments, delivered in writing. It is professional services work, not a financial product.

BCCS exists as a documented protocol layer — the Biological Compute & Clearing Standard — with public documentation at bccs.bio and deployed on-chain evidence on Base L2. Its verification unit, $BCCS, is treated as a protocol access and verification mechanism, not as a public investment product.

BioClearing exists as a conceptual, registry and clearing layer. It defines the settlement logic for verified biological state. Operational, production-scale use is not claimed at this time.

No public investment product is offered through KRYONIS Global. No public token sale is active. No production-scale biological verification network is claimed. Internal security review of BCCS contracts has been documented; an independent third-party smart-contract audit is pending and has not been completed.

V · What is not being claimed

Stated limits.

A proof layer is only as honest as its negative space. The following limits apply and take precedence over any language elsewhere that could be read otherwise.

  • KRYONIS Global is not a fund, a fund-like vehicle, or an asset manager.
  • KRYONIS Global is not offering securities in any jurisdiction.
  • KRYONIS Global is not conducting a public token sale.
  • $BCCS must not be described as a guaranteed investment asset. It is a protocol access and verification unit whose value is not guaranteed and may be zero.
  • No return, liquidity, validator earning, staking income or appreciation is implied by anything published by KRYONIS Global.
  • Any future token, license, validator, liquidity or protocol activation phase is conditional — subject to legal, technical and compliance review before any activation.
  • Internal review is not an independent third-party smart-contract audit. Until an external audit is published, no contract should be treated as independently audited.
