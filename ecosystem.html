The Ecosystem

KRYONIS is the meta-architecture. Each domain is sovereign within its layer,
operates on its own site, and connects through doctrine — not through hierarchy.

Conductor, not Orchestra

KRYONIS conducts.
It does not absorb.

The ecosystem has four layers. Each one is its own institution with its own audience, output and language. KRYONIS provides the shared intellectual frame; it does not collapse the layers into a single domain.

I · Doctrine · Engine · Map
kryonis.global
The Civilization Discovery Engine

The upper architecture. Doctrine, methodology, the seven-agent engine, the hypothesis registry, and the ecosystem map. Where the question of the next economy is held as a coherent intellectual instrument rather than as a collection of projects.

kryonis.global is the meta-layer. It does not publish technical specifications, perform clearing, or run protocols. It conducts the work of the other three domains and gives them their shared frame.

III · Protocol · Verification
bccs.bio
Biological Compute & Clearing Standard

The protocol layer. An immutable biological asset identifier, an eight-state lifecycle model for living systems, and the verification rail on which a bio-verifiable economy can actually run. Sovereign of any single jurisdiction by design.

BCCS is the technical instrument that the doctrine of kryonis.global anticipated. It is not the application; it is the standard the applications must respect.

IV · Application · Clearing
bioclearing.global
BioClearing

The applied clearing domain. Where the protocol meets practice: settlement of verified biological state across counterparties, jurisdictions, and time. The first operational expression of the verification-first economy described in the registry.

BioClearing is where doctrine, institution, and protocol arrive at the world. It is the layer at which the bioeconomy becomes a system other parties can transact within.

Discipline

Each layer is sovereign within itself.

KRYONIS does not consolidate. The institution publishes; the protocol verifies; the clearing settles. The doctrine names the gap each layer addresses but does not occupy any of their domains. This separation is structural, not stylistic — it is how the architecture earns the right to be civilizational rather than corporate.

A coherent architecture, deliberately distributed.
