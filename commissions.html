Procedure

How commissioning works.

1 · Write

Use the commission button on the instrument you need. State the question or terrain in a few sentences. No deck, no call required.

2 · Scope

Within 48 hours you receive a one-page written scope: what will be delivered, by which date, at the stated price. If the question is outside our doctrine, we say so plainly and decline.

3 · Confirm

You confirm by Stripe invoice from KRYONIS Sovereign Systems Limited (Hong Kong). Work begins the same day. Larger instruments are split 50% on confirmation, 50% on delivery.

Proof of Register

The standard of the work is public: the Hypothesis Registry, the BCCS document suite, and KRYONIS Lab. A sample brief is available on request.

Commissions are the near edge of the engine. Longer-form work — standing counsel, protocol pilots, institutional partnership, and sovereign mandates — continues to run through the engagement tracks.
